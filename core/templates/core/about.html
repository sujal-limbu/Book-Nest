BookNest began in a small apartment in Kathmandu, where our founder spent evenings surrounded by stacks of borrowed books and the quiet frustration of not finding the right title nearby. The idea was simple yet urgent: bring a curated, lovingly organized bookstore to every reader in Nepal — and eventually, the world.

We launched in 2021 with fewer than 200 titles and a handwritten inventory. Within a year, we had grown to thousands of books across every genre imaginable — from Nepali literature and poetry to global bestsellers, academic texts, and rare collector's editions.

"A book is not just paper and ink — it is someone's entire world, compressed and offered to you."

Today, BookNest serves thousands of readers across Nepal. We're proud to carry books that are hard to find elsewhere, and even prouder of the community of curious minds we've built around them. Every order that leaves our warehouse carries a little piece of that original apartment with it.

What We Believe

Principles That Guide
Every Page We Turn

Curation Over Quantity

We don't just stock books — we handpick titles that matter. Every book in our catalog has been chosen with care, so you spend less time searching and more time reading.

Reader-First Always

From easy navigation to fast delivery and hassle-free returns — every decision we make starts with one question: does this make a reader's life better?

Community & Culture

We support local authors, Nepali publishers, and indie voices. A portion of every sale goes toward literacy programs across Nepal.

Honest Pricing

Books should be accessible. We keep our margins fair, offer regular discounts, and believe that the cost of a book should never be a barrier to knowledge.

Swift & Safe Delivery

We treat your books the way you would — carefully packed, tracked, and delivered to your door in perfect condition.

Real Conversations

Our support team are readers, not scripts. When you reach out, you talk to a real person who loves books just as much as you do.
